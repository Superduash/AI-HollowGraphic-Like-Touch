# HoloTouch v1.0.0
### AI Hand Gesture Mouse Controller

> Control your PC with air gestures. Touchless, interactive, and AI-powered virtual mouse control.

---

## Key Features
- **Touchless Cursor Control:** Move your cursor smoothly by simply pointing at the screen.
- **Air Gestures:**
  - **Left Click / Drag:** Pinch your thumb and index finger.
  - **Right Click:** Pinch your thumb and middle finger.
  - **Scroll:** Raise both index and middle fingers together and move vertically.
- **Dual-Hand Support:** Swappable layouts (e.g., Right hand for cursor mapping, Left hand for clicks/scrolling).
- **Advanced Jitter Suppression:** EMA-smoothed cursor mapping keeps the pointer stable even at rest.
- **Immediate Stop Safeguards:** Automatically freezes cursor control and releases drag state when the tracking hand leaves the camera frame.

---

## How to Run
1. Double-click `HoloTouch.exe` to launch the application.
2. Grant camera permissions if requested.
3. Position yourself in front of the camera so that your hand is fully visible.
4. Access settings from the window menu to adjust sensitivity, smoothening, and control margins.

---

## Requirements
- **OS:** Windows 10 or 11 (64-bit)
- **Hardware:** USB webcam or integrated laptop camera
